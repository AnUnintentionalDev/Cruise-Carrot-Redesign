Cabins Overview

With a range of cabin types, ranging from the super-luxurious Executive Suites to elegant Oceanview Staterooms, the SuperStar Gemini will appeal to couples, families and groups of friends travelling together. Vibrant colour schemes and fine furnishings are used throughout.

Category of Cabins

Suites

Luxurious accommodation and bespoke service are the hallmarks of our suites. Designed to add a sense of grandeur to your cruise experience, our suites include picture windows and a spacious living area.

Balcony Cabins

For guests who yearn for some extra privacy, our handsome Balcony Cabins offer a generous outdoor space, a separate living area and plenty of luxurious touches, such as ensuite bathrooms, mini-fridges and flat-screen televisions.s

Oceanview Cabins

Our Oceanview Cabins offer guests the choice of either a window or porthole, both of which provide excellent ocean vistas. These stylish cabins are beautifully furnished and equipped with everything you might need on your voyage.

Inside Cabins

Bright, compact and packed with every comfort, our Inside Cabins offer a combination of beds and Pullman bunks. Cabins are ideal for short stay guests who plan to spend most of their time enjoying the ship and its facilities.
